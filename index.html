Questions

Frequently asked questions.

About the planner, tasks, analytics, AI assistant, notifications, and what's still coming.

How does the Weekly Planner decide what goes where?+

It looks at your task deadlines, priority levels, and how much time each one typically takes, then arranges study blocks across Monday to Sunday. You can drag and edit any block manually — the AI suggestion is a starting point, not a lock.

Can I edit or delete tasks after adding them?+

Yes. Every task in the Task Manager can be edited, marked complete, reprioritized, or deleted at any point. You can also filter by status and search across your full task history.

Where do the Analytics numbers come from?+

Directly from your own activity — completed and pending tasks, logged study hours, and priority distribution stored in SQLite via SQLAlchemy. Nothing is simulated; the dashboard reflects what you've actually done.

What can the AI Assistant actually help with?+

Study plan generation, explaining coding problems, assignment help, academic Q&A, time management advice, and career guidance. It's powered by Google Gemini and has context on your current tasks and deadlines.

What kinds of notifications will I get?+

Deadline alerts, task reminders, AI suggestions, study reminders, and upcoming exam notices. They surface inside the dashboard — no separate app to install or check.

When is the Internship Tracker launching?+

It's in active development. The plan covers application tracking, interview progress, offer letters, and resume readiness — built on the same data model as your academic dashboard. No date is locked yet, but it's the next major module.
