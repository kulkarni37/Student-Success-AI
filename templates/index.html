Import from your LMS, paste from WhatsApp groups, or enter manually. Student Success AI reads syllabi, notices hidden dependencies, and builds your complete academic picture in seconds.

The AI engine scores every task by deadline urgency, effort estimate, and grade weight. It maps your cognitive load curve across the week and flags collision points before you see them.

✦ FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

Is Student Success AI free to use? +

Yes — you can start planning for free with up to 10 assignments and full AI recommendations. Pro plans unlock unlimited tasks, the Deadline Simulator, Placement Tracker, and priority AI access.

Does it work for engineering students in India? +

Absolutely. Student Success AI was built with Indian engineering curricula in mind — including semester exams, mid-terms, lab submissions, mini projects, and campus placements. It understands your academic calendar.

How does the AI know how long each task will take? +

It learns from your historical completion patterns, difficulty ratings you assign, and aggregate data across the student community. Over two weeks it personalizes estimates to within 15% accuracy.

Can I use it on my phone? +

Yes. Student Success AI is fully responsive and works beautifully on mobile. A native app for iOS and Android is coming in Q3.
